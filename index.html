Bikeview.CA represents a video perspective on biking in Canada's Capital City, Ottawa, Ontario.

I walk, run, drive and yes, ride a bike(s)

This site is meant to capture some of the discussions and tidbits around cycling, mostly in Ottawa. Some of the pages will simply be lists of tweets on a particular part of driving, cycling, walking, whatever. Some will be links or embedded saved documents on city building, roads, transit, infrastructure, etc.

If you have a github account and want to help update the various twitter files send me a message on Twitter.
