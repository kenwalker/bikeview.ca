Too fast, causes death!

There are numerous forms of the stunt driving charge, but the easiest to apply is for speeding at least 50 km/h over the limit. Then there is an immediate 7 day license suspension, 7 day impound of the vehicle, minimum $2000 and likely a summon to appear in court.
