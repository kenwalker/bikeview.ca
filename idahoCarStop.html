Vehicles not stopping at stop signs, just in case you need a list

I'm in favour of the bicycle Idaho stop. That is to say, tread stop signs as yields. Doesn't mean blowing through them, just means you don't have to come to a complete stop and put your foot down. I cyclist requires a significant amount of energy to get back to speed and a cyclist has a phenominal field of view to see what's happening. A vehicle has many blind spots.
This list is grown manually but you can help by using the #IdahoCarStop hashtag and I'll search for that from time to time.
